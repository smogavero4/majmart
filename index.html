Custom Skateboard Decks

Custom skateboard paintings by MAJMart are each designed and hand painted on a wood skateboard deck with premium acrylic paints. These boards are covered in a thick layer of epoxy resin for increased durability and for preservation of the beautiful one-of-a-kind artwork. Hang these 3D pieces in any manner of spaces from a bedroom to a living room for a unique focal point.
